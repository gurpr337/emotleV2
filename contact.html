Contact Us

Have a question, feedback, or found a bug? We'd love to hear from you! At Emotle, we believe in constant improvement, and our community's input is the driving force behind the features we build.

How to Reach Us

Whether you have a suggestion for a new game mode, found a technical issue, or just want to tell us how much you're enjoying the daily puzzles, you can reach out through the following channels:

  • Official Email: hello@emotle.com

Bug Reporting

If you're reporting a bug, it helps us tremendously if you can include your device type (iPhone, Android, PC), the browser you were using (Safari, Chrome, etc.), and a brief description of what happened. This allows our small team to replicate and fix issues much faster.

Puzzle Suggestions

While we have a dedicated Submission Page for emoji phrases, you can also email us your creative ideas directly if you have multiple suggestions or want to discuss a thematic week!

We try to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. Thank you for being an active part of the Emotle community!
