How to Play Emotle

Welcome to Emotle! Your goal is to deduce the hidden word or phrase represented by the emojis at the top of the screen. You have 5 attempts to get it right.

The Basic Rules

1. Analyze the emojis: They are your primary clues. They might represent an object, an action, a famous person, or a common idiom.

2. Type your guess: Each guess must be a valid word of the specified length.

3. Submit: Press the "Submit" button on the game keyboard to check your guess.
