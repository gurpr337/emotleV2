Emotle

How to Play

Guess the EMOTLE in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid word or phrase (though currently we focus on single words). Hit the enter button to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Emoji Phrasing

Pay close attention to the emoji displayed at the top. It's your primary clue! The emoji represents the concept, action, or object you're trying to guess.
