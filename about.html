About Emotle

Emotle is a daily word-guessing game with a twist: instead of just guessing a word, you're translating a story told through emojis.

Inspired by the simplicity of Wordle and the expressive power of emojis, Emotle challenges players to think creatively and symbolically. Every day, a new "emoji phrase" is selected, and players have five attempts to guess the hidden word or phrase it represents.

Our mission is to provide a quick, fun, and mentally stimulating break in your day. Whether you're an emoji expert or just looking for a new puzzle, Emotle is designed for everyone.

How it started

Emotle began as a side project to explore how much meaning we can pack into a few tiny icons. It quickly grew into a daily ritual for a community of puzzle lovers worldwide.
