Frequently Asked Questions

When is a new Emotle released?

A new puzzle is released every day at midnight local time.

Why did my streak reset?

Streaks are stored in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser data or switch devices, your streak will reset. We recommend playing on the same device and browser to maintain your streak.

Are there more than one word phrases?

Currently, the daily puzzle is a single word. Future updates may include multi-word phrases!

Can I play past puzzles?

Yes! Check out our Archive or Practice Mode to play puzzles you've missed.
